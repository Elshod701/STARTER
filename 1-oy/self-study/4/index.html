Glittering Bublanina

About

Glittering Bublanina is a delicious and easy-to-make dessert that is perfect for any occasion. It is made with a simple batter of eggs, sugar, flour, and baking powder, and it is then topped with a sparkling sugar glaze.

This recipe is for a classic vanilla-flavored bublanina, but you can also experiment with other flavors, such as chocolate, lemon, or strawberry.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup sparkling sugar, for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and eggs until light and fluffy. Beat in the vanilla extract.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients alternately with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Stir in the oil until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with sparkling sugar.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool completely before serving.

Tips

  • For a more intense flavor, use a good quality vanilla extract.
  • If you don't have sparkling sugar, you can use regular granulated sugar. Just add a pinch of salt to the sugar before sprinkling it on top of the cake.
  • This cake is best served warm, but it can also be served at room temperature.
